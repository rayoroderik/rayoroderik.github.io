Privacy Policy for Padelicano

Last Updated: May 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Padelicano. We respect your privacy and want to be transparent about our approach to data handling. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is managed when you use our Padelicano application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Padelicano App, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Data Storage and Collection

Padelicano does not collect any of your information. All data is stored locally on your device only.

The types of information stored locally include:

  • Player names, gender, and profiles you create
  • Tournament data, including match results and scores
  • Tournament configurations and settings

This data is used solely within the app to manage your tournaments and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

Local Data Usage

The information stored locally on your device is used only for:

  • Managing tournaments and tracking scores
  • Creating and maintaining player profiles
  • Generating leaderboards and statistics
  • Improving your experience with the Padelicano App

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data security depends on your device's security settings
  • Data backup is your responsibility through your device's backup mechanisms
  • Data will remain on your device until you uninstall the app or clear the app data

User Control Over Data

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete tournaments, players, or other data directly within the app
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device
  • You can use your device's settings to clear the app's data

Third-Party Services

Padelicano does not use third-party services that collect your information. All functionality is handled locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
