Privacy Policy for Padelicano

Last Updated: May 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Padelicano. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Padelicano application ("App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Padelicano App, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and gender when you register or create player profiles
  • Tournament Data: Match results, scores, player statistics, and tournament rankings
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent on various screens

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Managing tournaments and tracking scores
  • Creating and maintaining player profiles
  • Generating leaderboards and statistics
  • Improving and optimizing the Padelicano App
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Sending important notifications about tournaments or app updates

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Tournament data is backed up regularly
  • We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy
  • Personal data will be stored until you request its deletion or close your account

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection features
  • Export your tournament history and statistics

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the email address listed in the Contact Information section.

Third-Party Services

Padelicano may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Analytics services to improve app performance
  • Cloud storage providers for data backup
  • Email services for notifications

These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

Children's Privacy

Padelicano is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
