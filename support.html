Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new tournament?
To create a new tournament, tap the "Create tournament" button on the main screen. Enter the tournament name, select the tournament type (Americano, Mexicano, or Mixicano), set points per round, and add player information.
What are the different tournament types?

Americano: Players rotate partners after each round, allowing everyone to play with and against different people.

Mexicano: Players are paired based on their current ranking, with stronger players facing stronger opponents.

Mixicano: A hybrid format combining elements of both Americano and Mexicano, with an emphasis on mixed gender pairings.

How does scoring work?
Each match is played to a predetermined number of points (by default 24). Players accumulate points individually across all rounds. The player with the highest total points at the end of the tournament is the winner.
How do I add or remove players?
Players can be added during tournament creation. Currently, players cannot be added or removed once a tournament has started to maintain score integrity.

Troubleshooting Tips

Tournament Creation Issues

  • Ensure you have at least 4 players added
  • For Mixicano tournaments, verify you have an equal number of male and female players
  • Check that all required fields are completed

Score Tracking Problems

  • If scores are not updating, try exiting the match view and returning
  • Ensure you have the latest app version
  • Verify the total match points do not exceed the points per round setting
